  • Cannabis

    Cannabis refers to the
    dried leaves, flowers, stems,
    and seeds of the cannabis plant

  • Cocaine

    an addictive stimulant
    drug made from the leaves
    of the coca plant

  • Heroin

    an opioid drug made from morphine,
    a natural substance taken from the
    seed pod of the various opium poppy plants

  • Tobacco

    Tobacco is a plant with leaves
    that contain nicotine,
    a highly addictive chemical.
